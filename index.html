The visualization provides a global representation of Air Quality Index (AQI) values for countries around the world. Each country is color-coded to reflect its AQI category, with gradients ranging from "Good" to "Hazardous" based on air quality levels. By hovering the mouse over a specific country, users can view its precise AQI value, offering an interactive and detailed exploration of global air quality patterns.

The Air Quality Index (AQI) is a critical measure of air pollution and its potential impact on human health. It translates complex air quality data into a simple scale that categorizes conditions from "Good" to "Hazardous." High AQI values indicate unhealthy air that can cause respiratory issues, aggravate chronic diseases, and increase health risks for sensitive populations such as children and the elderly. Monitoring AQI helps individuals and policymakers make informed decisions to protect public health and implement measures to improve air quality globally.
